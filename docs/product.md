# Griit

Griit is a modern SaaS that lets athletes create a beautiful public profile
centered on their next goal.

The product is NOT a social network.

The product is NOT a Strava competitor.

Think of it as "Linktree for athletes", but much richer.

Users create a page like:

griit.me/username

that they can share on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube or anywhere online.

The goal is not to collect followers.

The goal is to build an athlete identity around a clear objective.

Every profile should tell a story.

Every profile should make the athlete's next goal obvious in the first screen.

---

## Vision

Every athlete has a story.

Every athlete has a next objective.

Every athlete has achievements.

Every athlete deserves a beautiful public profile.

Griit helps athletes build their online identity around goals, activities,
milestones, achievements, photos and personal information.

Profiles can include current objectives such as running 10K under 40 minutes,
finishing a marathon, preparing for a boxing fight on a specific date, or any
other sport-specific target.

Public profiles use templates. The default template highlights the next goal,
and future templates can change the structure and visual direction of the page.
Some templates are available only on the Pro plan.
